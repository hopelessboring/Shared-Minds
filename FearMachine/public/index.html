Fear Machine

Neural Networks function by modeling the neuron-based connections in our

brains. Explore how machine learning models can simulate synaptic firing,

generating patterns that lead to new ideas and aspects of complex thought.


Every time you hit the button an ML model populates a new, very real human fear.

The model then maps that idea on the canvas at a point that is relevant to the implicit meaning behind the idea.

Asmore and more fears are animated to the canvas, similar ideas will start to aggregate in clouds.
